德·摩根律 德摩根律：（非常重要）长杠变短杠，开口换方向
1. A AND B=NOT ((NOT A) OR (NOT B))
2. A OR B=NOT ((NOT A) AND (NOT B))
demo:
SELECT * FROM student_01 WHERE name='李四3' AND score=80
SELECT * FROM student_01 WHERE NOT((NOT name='李四3') OR (NOT score=80))
